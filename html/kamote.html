Kamote Cue

Ingredients

  • Sweet potatoes (kamote), sliced
  • Brown sugar
  • Cooking oil
  • Bamboo skewers

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a deep pan.
  2. Fry sweet potato slices until half cooked.
  3. Sprinkle brown sugar into the hot oil; let it caramelize.
  4. Continue frying sweet potatoes until coated with caramelized sugar and fully cooked.
  5. Skewer pieces onto bamboo sticks.
  6. Serve hot as a sweet snack.

History

Kamote Cue is a beloved Tigwahanon street-style snack, symbolizing joy and community gatherings.
