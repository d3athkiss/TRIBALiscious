Utan Bisaya

Ingredients

  • Mixed vegetables (okra, eggplant, squash, malunggay leaves)
  • Tomatoes and onions
  • Salt or fish sauce
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Bring water to a boil in a pot.
  2. Add onions and tomatoes; simmer until softened.
  3. Add vegetables starting with the firm ones (squash, eggplant), then softer greens (okra, malunggay).
  4. Season with salt or fish sauce.
  5. Simmer until vegetables are tender but not mushy.
  6. Serve hot as a healthy side dish.

History

Utan Bisaya is a simple vegetable stew, reflecting the Higaonon tribe’s reliance on fresh produce and healthy living.
