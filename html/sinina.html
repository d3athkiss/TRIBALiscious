Sinina

Ingredients

  • Beef or carabao meat, cubed
  • Garlic, onion, ginger
  • Spices (pepper, turmeric, chili optional)
  • Coconut milk (thin and thick)
  • Salt and oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pot. Sauté garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add beef/carabao cubes. Brown on all sides.
  3. Pour in thin coconut milk. Season with salt and spices. Simmer 30–40 minutes until meat is tender.
  4. Add thick coconut milk. Stir gently and simmer 10 minutes more until sauce is rich.
  5. Taste and adjust seasoning. Serve hot with rice.

History

Sinina is a ceremonial Manobo stew, often served at weddings and tribal gatherings to honor guests.
