Inasal nga Manok

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Garlic, vinegar, soy sauce
  • Annatto oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in garlic, vinegar, soy sauce, salt, and pepper for 1–2 hours.
  2. Prepare grill with hot coals.
  3. Grill chicken, basting regularly with annatto oil.
  4. Cook until golden and slightly charred, about 30 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with rice and dipping sauce.

History

Inasal nga Manok is a Talaandig festive grilled chicken dish, often prepared for celebrations and communal feasts.
