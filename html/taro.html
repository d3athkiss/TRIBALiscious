Boiled Taro

Ingredients

  • Taro roots
  • Water
  • Salt (optional)

Instructions

  1. Wash taro roots thoroughly and peel.
  2. Cut into chunks.
  3. Place in a pot with water and bring to a boil.
  4. Simmer until taro is tender, about 20–30 minutes.
  5. Drain and sprinkle with salt if desired.
  6. Serve warm, plain or with coconut.

History

Boiled taro is a staple food of the Umayamnon tribe, eaten simply to sustain energy during daily work.
