Sinuglaw

Ingredients

  • Grilled pork belly
  • Fresh fish (for kinilaw/ceviche)
  • Vinegar or calamansi juice
  • Onion, ginger, chili
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Grill pork belly until charred and cooked, then slice into cubes.
  2. Prepare fish ceviche: cube fish, marinate in vinegar or calamansi juice with onion, ginger, chili, salt, and pepper for 10–15 minutes.
  3. Combine grilled pork and fish ceviche in a bowl.
  4. Toss gently to mix flavors.
  5. Serve immediately with rice or as pulutan.

History

Sinuglaw combines “sinugba” (grilled) and “kinilaw” (ceviche), showing Higaonon creativity in blending cooking methods.
