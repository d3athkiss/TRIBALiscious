Wild Yam

Ingredients

  • Wild yam tubers
  • Water
  • Salt (optional)

Instructions

  1. Scrub and peel wild yam tubers.
  2. Cut into chunks.
  3. Boil in water until tender, about 20–30 minutes.
  4. Drain and sprinkle with salt if desired.
  5. Serve plain or with grated coconut.

History

Wild yam is a survival food of the Higaonon tribe, gathered from forests and eaten boiled for sustenance.
