Inaloban

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Coconut milk (thin and thick)
  • Garlic, onion, ginger
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil for sautéing

Instructions

  1. Sauté garlic, onion, and ginger in oil until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken pieces and brown lightly.
  3. Pour in thin coconut milk, season with salt and pepper, and simmer until chicken is half cooked.
  4. Add thick coconut milk and continue simmering until chicken is tender and sauce is creamy.
  5. Serve hot with rice.

History

Inaloban is a coconut-based chicken dish of the Umayamnon tribe, highlighting their use of native ingredients.
