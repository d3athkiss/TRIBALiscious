L'lulot na Baboy

Ingredients

  • Pork chunks
  • Banana leaves
  • Garlic, onion, ginger
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Clean and cut pork into chunks.
  2. Season with salt, pepper, and spices.
  3. Wrap pork tightly in banana leaves.
  4. Steam for 45–60 minutes until tender.
  5. Unwrap and serve hot with rice.

History

L’lulot na Baboy is a Talaandig ritual dish, symbolizing respect for nature and community feasting.
