Ginamos

Ingredients

  • Small fish (anchovy-like)
  • Salt
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Clean small fish and drain.
  2. Mix fish with generous salt in a jar.
  3. Cover and let ferment for 5–7 days.
  4. Stir occasionally to ensure even fermentation.
  5. Serve as condiment with rice or vegetables.

History

Ginamos is a fermented fish paste, a staple condiment of the Matigsalug tribe, valued for preservation.
