Binugsong na Manok

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Banana leaves
  • Garlic, onion, ginger
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and spices.
  2. Wrap chicken tightly in banana leaves.
  3. Steam parcels for 30–40 minutes until chicken is cooked.
  4. Unwrap and serve hot with rice.

History

Binugsong na Manok is a celebratory Matigsalug dish, symbolizing abundance and tradition.
