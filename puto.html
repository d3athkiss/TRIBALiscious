Puto Balanghoy

Ingredients

  • Grated cassava
  • Sugar
  • Grated coconut
  • Banana leaves for lining

Instructions

  1. Prepare banana leaves: Wash, cut squares, and lightly heat to soften.
  2. Mix grated cassava with sugar and coconut in a bowl.
  3. Spoon mixture onto banana leaves and fold into parcels.
  4. Arrange parcels in a steamer. Steam 20–30 minutes until firm.
  5. Cool slightly, then serve warm with extra grated coconut.

History

Puto Balanghoy is a cassava cake enjoyed by the Manobo tribe, often shared during community events and celebrations.
