Linotlot

Ingredients

  • Native chicken pieces
  • Banana leaves
  • Garlic, onion, ginger
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and spices.
  2. Wrap chicken tightly in banana leaves.
  3. Steam parcels for 40–50 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
  4. Unwrap and serve hot with rice.

History

Linotlot is a Tigwahanon specialty, prepared for communal feasts and symbolizing unity.
