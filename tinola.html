Native Chicken Tinola

Ingredients

  • Native chicken, cut into pieces
  • Green papaya or sayote
  • Malunggay leaves
  • Garlic, onion, ginger
  • Fish sauce or salt
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Sauté garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in water and bring to a boil. Simmer until chicken is tender.
  4. Add green papaya or sayote; cook until softened.
  5. Season with fish sauce or salt.
  6. Add malunggay leaves just before serving.
  7. Serve hot with rice.

History

Native Chicken Tinola is a comforting Tigwahanon dish, often served to family and guests as a symbol of care.
