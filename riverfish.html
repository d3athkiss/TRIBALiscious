Umbrian Fish Soup

Ingredients

  • Fresh river fish
  • Onion, garlic, ginger
  • Leafy greens
  • Salt and pepper
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Clean fish and cut into pieces.
  2. Boil water in a pot with onion, garlic, and ginger.
  3. Add fish and simmer until cooked through.
  4. Add leafy greens and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Simmer briefly until greens are tender.
  6. Serve hot as a nourishing soup.

History

Umbrian Fish Soup is a traditional Umayamnon dish, reflecting their reliance on river resources for sustenance.
