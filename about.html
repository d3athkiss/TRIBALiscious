Our Goal

TRIBALiscious was created to preserve and celebrate the culinary heritage of Bukidnon’s 7 tribes. We aim to document traditional recipes, share their cultural significance, and inspire pride in indigenous identity. By showcasing these dishes online, we help future generations learn, cook, and connect with their roots.

How This Website Helps

  • Preserve: Safeguard recipes and rituals from being forgotten.
  • Educate: Teach younger generations about the meaning behind each dish.
  • Promote: Share tribal culture with a wider audience, fostering respect and awareness.
  • Connect: Provide a space where tribes can showcase their heritage proudly.
