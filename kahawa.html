Kahawa Sug

Ingredients

  • Coffee beans
  • Water
  • Sugar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Roast coffee beans in a pan until fragrant and dark.
  2. Cool slightly, then grind coarsely.
  3. Boil water in a pot. Add ground coffee.
  4. Simmer 5–10 minutes. Strain if desired.
  5. Serve hot, sweetened with sugar if preferred.

History

Kahawa Sug is a traditional Manobo coffee, symbolizing hospitality and warmth in gatherings.
