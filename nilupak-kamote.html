Nilupak nga Kamote

Ingredients

  • Sweet potatoes
  • Sugar
  • Grated coconut
  • Butter or coconut oil (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil sweet potatoes until tender, then peel.
  2. Mash or pound until smooth.
  3. Mix in sugar and grated coconut. Add butter/oil if desired.
  4. Shape into logs or patties.
  5. Serve on banana leaves, topped with extra coconut.

History

Nilupak nga Kamote is a Talaandig comfort food, highlighting their agricultural roots and communal traditions.
