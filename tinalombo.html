Tinalombo

Ingredients

  • Fresh fish (tilapia or river fish)
  • Banana leaves
  • Salt and pepper
  • Spices (ginger, garlic, onion)

Instructions

  1. Clean fish thoroughly and pat dry.
  2. Season with salt, pepper, and spices.
  3. Wrap fish securely in banana leaves.
  4. Grill over charcoal until cooked through, about 15–20 minutes.
  5. Serve hot, still wrapped, for smoky flavor.

History

Tinalombo reflects the Matigsalug tribe’s river culture, using fresh fish wrapped in leaves for grilling.
